Research Interests

Broadly, I am interested in arithmetic geometry. More specifically, my current research focuses on:

  • The geometry of moduli spaces of sheaves.
  • Rational points on algebraic varieties.
  • Applications of the Langlands program.

An example of an object I frequently study is the local zeta function, defined for a variety $X$ over a finite field $\mathbb{F}_q$ as:

$$Z(X/\mathbb{F}_q, t) = \exp\left( \sum_{m=1}^{\infty} N_m \frac{t^m}{m} \right)$$

where $N_m$ denotes the number of points on $X$ defined over the extension $\mathbb{F}_{q^m}$.
